About the movie

After the infamous der Schuh des Manitu, "Bully" is back again with this delightful parody of Starwars, Startrek and Back to the Future which will have you rolling on the floor laughing. The movie is based on a recurring skit from Herbig's TV show, Bullyparade. This German comedy film has been granted numerous awards like the prestigious Bambi award. Starring Til Schweiger, Michael Herbig, Christian Tramitz, Anja Kling, Rick Kavanian, and Sky du Mont.

Synopsis
Hundreds of years after humans have settled on Mars, Regulator Rogul and Lord Jens Maul, lead a force of Martians to Earth in order to conquer the planet. Queen Metaphor looks to the gay heroes aboard the spaceship Surprise – Captain Kork, Mr. Spuck, and first engineer Schrotty
